Meet our Chefs

Chef Alessandra

Chef Alessandra brings a touch of Italy to our kitchen, with a culinary journey shaped by her Sicilian roots. Her passion for traditional flavors and innovative techniques results in dishes that tantalize the taste buds, making her a maestro in crafting authentic Italian cuisine

Chef Raj

With a background in fusion cuisine, Chef Raj blends diverse culinary influences to create a symphony of flavors on the plate. Trained in both Eastern and Western culinary arts, Raj's inventive approach showcases a harmonious marriage of spices and ingredients, promising a culinary adventure with every bite.

Chef Javier

Chef Javier, a master of Latin American cuisine, infuses his dishes with the vibrant and bold flavors of his Mexican heritage. His commitment to using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients ensures an authentic and sustainable dining experience, while his creativity transforms traditional recipes into contemporary culinary delights.

Chef Mei

our expert in Asian gastronomy, brings a wealth of knowledge and skill from her culinary journey across the continent. Specializing in the delicate art of sushi and the bold flavors of Chinese cuisine, Mei's creations showcase a perfect balance of tradition and innovation, making her a standout in the world of Pan-Asian culinary excellence.
